Guru Nanak Dev Ji

Born: April 15, 1469

Gurgadi Diwas: N/A (First Guru)

Jyoti Jot Diwas: September 22, 1539

Father: Mehta Kalu

Mother: Mata Tripta

Wife: Mata Sulakhni

Children: Sri Chand, Lakhmi Das

Guru Nanak Dev Ji was the founder of Sikhism. He preached a message of love, equality, and devotion to One God. His teachings laid the foundation of the Sikh faith and continue to inspire millions around the world.
